About

Programmer extraordinaire

I'm a 24-year-old programmer extraordinaire residing in Calgary, Canada. I specialize in building polished and secure Web sites using DJango and jQuery, though I also work with numerous other languages and frameworks, including PHP and Ruby on Rails. My programming acumen is equalled only by my Linux and BSD sysadmin skills, which garner considerable respect on the streets.

I have a painstaking eye for detail.

Computers are fussy about the little things, and as someone who speaks their language, so am I. When you work with me, you can rest easy knowing that every important detail will be meticulously massaged until it's just right.

I'm pragmatic.

Though I strive for technical perfection, I understand that my code's purpose is to serve your wider goals. After all, my work is at its best when your user never notices it.

My sysadmin-fu is strong.

I've administered a small cluster of Linux systems through the last six years, running Gentoo, Arch, Debian, and Ubuntu, in addition to an OpenBSD firewall. I've deployed technologies including Xen, Apache, lighttpd, nginx, Django, Ruby on Rails, PHP, PostgreSQL, Dovecot, Postfix, Pure-FTPd, vsftpd, TeamSpeak, and PF.

I build secure systems.

Nothing strikes fear into my heart quite like the notion of a coding or configuration error leading to a security breach. Only by focussing on security at every stage of development can I sleep soundly through the night.

I learn new things quickly.

Software developers must continually adapt to new technologies. If I must reach outside my existing skillset to create the best possible result, I will promptly learn what I need to know, then immediately put it into practice.
